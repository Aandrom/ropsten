Aandrom - ZIR

Aandrom is the first Zero Investment Risk ERC20 token,
designed so that its price potentially rises.

ZIR - Zero Investment Risk is new ERC20 possibility that allow
users get invested ether back before sale end.User can active ZIR only if he just bought
Aandrom one time.If user bought Aandrom more then one he lose possibility to
get invested ether back,or if user do anything with Aandrom.Example: If user send
Aandrom to any address,give approve,put coins in staking...

20M tokens are locked.They can be minted only staking.It is designed so that the price potentially rises
because people lock up tokens and circulate supply will be less.Staking is possible only if total circulate coins is less then 30M.Where
circulate reach 30M of Aandrom tokens,staking will be disabled.The most important thing: Locked tokens will get
1% reward per day.If the agreement is violated and the tokens are withdrawn before the agreed time, 10% is deducted.

There are 10M tokens in sale.Total supply is 30M.After sales end all participants
who are bought more then 1 time tokens can take all unsold tokens.The tokens are
divided equally, everyone gets the same number of tokens.
